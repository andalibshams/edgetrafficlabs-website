Our long-term vision

The operating layer for intelligent transportation.

We envision a foundational middleware platform that standardizes how sensors, controllers, analytics tools, and intelligent transportation applications work together.

With default implementations at every layer and well-defined APIs for customization, agencies and innovators can build, test, and deploy new capabilities without sacrificing interoperability, reliability, or maintainability.
